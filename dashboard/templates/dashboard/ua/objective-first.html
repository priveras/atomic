Set Objective

When setting objectives you have to be specific. Use SMARTobjectives

Also, you need to know who the customer is before setting your objective.

Example and Tips

SMART Objective Structure:

Our goal this year/quarter/month is to (INCREASE OR DECREASE) (X) by/to (PLUG IN A SPECIFIC %/PLUG IN A SPECIFIC NUMBER OR COUNT) in order to (BLANK (SHOULD BE SPECIFIC & TIED TO BUSINESS MODEL)).

Example SMART Objective:

Convert 33% of leads with customers within 30 days of initial contact.

Define KPIs

With metrics, think broad. With key performance indicators, think deep.

Example and Tips

KPI: A measurable value that demonstrates how effectively a company is achieving key business objectives. Without establishing and tracking proper key performance indicators, companies would be left in the dark about their performance

Metric: A quantifiable measure that is used to track and assess the status of a specific process. Metrics are important because they are comprised of a wide swathe of all trackable areas.
