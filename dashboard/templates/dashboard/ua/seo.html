Search Engine Optimization

Use this tool to work with search engine optimization for your site

What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the practice of improving and promoting a website, in order to increase the number of (non-paid) visitors the site receives from search engines.

Why is it important?

Search is a high-intent channel. If you are a user on a search engine, you are actively looking for a solution to a problem. If your business is not found, it is losing revenue.

Why does it matter?

SEO matters because it’s a high intent marketing channel. That means that whoever is performing the search, is looking for an answer to a question, therefore the probability for conversion is much higher.

In simple terms, search is the channel that has the better quality of visitors and the highest probability for convertion.

How do I optimize for search engines?

Basically you just have to build your site to answer questions and have the right setup to allow search engines to find your awesome content.

If you have a page that has your address, you have to ask yourself. What question is that answering? If you have an “about me” page, it has to answer questions about “me”.

Your answers have to be RELEVANT , UNIQUE and RICH IN CONTENT.

Example: To make an address page more rich, relevant and unique, you can add an address and contact number, a google maps link and a map image.

If you provide answers to those questions you’re going to be ranked higher simply because you become more relevant to whoever performed the search.
